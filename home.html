Early life

The God-Emperor of Mankind is the strongest Psyker ever born to Humanity, possibly being the only non-heretical immortala being. His immortality surpasses the kind we see in machine-spirits and even daemons.
He is the creator and father of the Primarchs.
In the 30th millennium, he gloriously united the Old Earth we now call Terra. He regrets the many innocent deaths as a necessary sacrifice for the greater good of unifying Humanity to protect it from the manifestations from the Warp.
