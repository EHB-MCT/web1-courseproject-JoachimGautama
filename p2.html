The Protector of Humanity

Throughout his reign, the Emperor’s primary goal was the preservation of humanity. His creation of the Astronomican—a psychic beacon for interstellar travel—enabled the Imperium’s vast expansion and kept humanity united. His psychic powers continue to protect the human race even now, shielding them from the daemonic horrors of the Warp.
